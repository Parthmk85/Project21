## Usage

### Navigation

- Click navigation links to smoothly scroll to sections
- Mobile: Use hamburger menu for navigation

### Shopping

1. Browse products or filter by category
2. Click product card to view details
3. Click "+" button or "Add to Cart" to add items
4. Click cart icon to view/manage cart
5. Adjust quantities or remove items in cart
6. Click "Proceed to Checkout" (demo only)

### Forms

- **Contact Form**: Fill out and submit (console logs data)
- **Newsletter**: Enter email to subscribe (console logs data)
